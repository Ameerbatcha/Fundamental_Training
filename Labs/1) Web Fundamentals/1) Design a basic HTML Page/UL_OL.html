Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Khans Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallows (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in he microwave to just below a boil
  3. Pour milk into the mug, stirring gently as you pour
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolve in the milk.
  5. Top with marshmallows if you must
